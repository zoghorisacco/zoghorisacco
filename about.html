HISTORY

"Formation of like-minded individuals to help transform the community through nurturing a savings culture provision of accessible, affordable and low-interest rate loans"

Zoghori Sacco was established in 2004 and registered in the year 2005 with the main aim of improving the lifestyle of the community through inculcating a saving culture for prosperity and to liberate the community from being that of a consumer in nature by offering quick access to cheap and affordable credit facilities.

As a result, funds mobilized provide cheap access to credit facilities, financial education and bring awareness on nurturing a saving culture; personal financial management and identification of opportunities and investments.

Our Historical Progress

The SACCO has experienced a steady and robust growth over the years since its inception in the year 2004

This has been achieved because all our programs are geared towards helping to:

  • Help nurture a saving and investment culture to enable communities to come out of consumer bracket.
  • Provide affordable, easy to access loans to registered members
  • Provide affordable plots/housing loans for low and moderate income individuals
  • Promote economic development by financing small business or small firms
  • Provide job training for low and moderate income individuals
  • Provide financial education and promote economic empowerment
  • Revitalize low and moderate income communities.

Membership

There is membership growth every single day, people are becoming a part of something magnificent because of the introduction of the Open Bond

Our membership has been growing by leaps ever since inception. The membership is open and drawn from all the socio-economic dimensions to give an opportunity for all and sundry to experience growth financially and economically

Leadership

It is the strong leadership at the helm of the institution that has enabled it to continue growing steadily

The SACCO is governed by an Annual Delegates Meeting where crucial resolutions are drafted and adopted and the mandate of execution granted unto the elected Board of Directors. The Board of Directors appoints and employs employees to help them run the SACCO.

Zoghori Foundation

Our commitment is in building people's lives through progressive transformation

Zoghori Foundation is a non-profit implementing foundation set up in 2019 as the social arm of Zoghori Sacco Limited with the aim of transforming the lives and livelihoods of our people socially and economically by availing modern, inclusive opportunities that improve and help build and strengthen their prosperity.
