M-PESA Pay Bill Payment Process

Business Number: 4077167 | Account Format: [Identity Number]#

How to Make a Payment via M-PESA Pay Bill

Follow these simple steps to make a payment to our SACCO using M-PESA:

  1. Open your M-PESA menu on your mobile phone. This can be accessed via the SIM Toolkit, Safaricom App, or by dialing *334#.
  2. Select Lipa na M-PESA from the menu options.
  3. Choose Pay Bill.
  4. Enter the Business Number: 4077167.
  5. Enter your Account Number in the format: [Your Identity Number]#.
    Example: If your ID number is 12345678, input 12345678#.
  6. Enter the Amount you wish to pay (e.g., KSh 1,000).
  7. Input your M-PESA PIN and press OK.
  8. Confirm the details and press Send. You’ll receive a confirmation SMS from M-PESA once the transaction is complete.
Important Notes
  • Ensure your Identity Number is correct and followed by a hash (#) toavoid transaction errors.
  • The Business Number 4077167 is specific to our SACCO. Double-check before sending.
  • Transaction limits apply: Minimum KSh 1, Maximum KSh 150,000 per transaction (subject to M-PESA limits).
  • For assistance, contact our SACCO support at info@yoursacco.org or call 0700 123 456.
Example Transaction

Scenario: John Doe, ID 12345678, wants to pay KSh 2,000.

  • M-PESA Menu → Lipa na M-PESA → Pay Bill
  • Business Number: 4077167
  • Account Number: 12345678#
  • Amount: 2000
  • Enter PIN → Confirm → Send

John receives an SMS confirming payment to SACCO Business No. 4077167.
