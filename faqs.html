Zoghori Sacco FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About: Membership, Loans, Leadership, and More

Membership

All Kenyan individual aged 18+ who resides within the borders of Kenya, or is a Kenyan but lives in the Diaspora. Persons from the following categories are all eligible for membership:
  • Public Service employees; Goverment Institutions/Parastatals.
  • Employees of Zoghori Sacco Society
  • Employees in Private Companies and NGO’s
  • Self –Employed Individuals
  • elf-Help Registered Groups and Community Based Organizations.
  • Corporate Bodies and Companies

You need a copy of your National ID, a recent passport-size photo, and a completed application form available at our offices or website submitted together with the Next of Kin or Nominee copies of Identity Documents.

Membership approval takes less than 20 minutes during working hours after submitting all required documents and fees.

Membership is non-transferable, but you can nominate a beneficiary for your savings and shares in case of exit or demise.

Loans

We offer Development Loans, Emergency Loans, School Fees Loans, and Business Loans, each with specific terms and conditions.

You must be a member for 6 months, have savings 3x the loan amount, and provide 2 guarantors who are active members.

Rates range from 1% to 1.5% per month on a reducing balance, depending on the loan type and term.

Loan approvals take 5-10 working days after submitting a complete application.

Leadership

The Sacco is managed by a Board of Directors elected by members during the Annual General Meeting (AGM).

Elections occur every 2 years at the AGM. Members nominate and vote for candidates who meet leadership criteria.
