CREDIT PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

    Development Loan

    This is a loan advanced to a member who wants to undertake a minor or major project in any field such as business startup capital, residential or commercial construction, shares or stock investment, lpo or tender financing, etc.

    Terms & Conditions
  • Member's account MUST be ACTIVE.
  • Loan is issued on Member's Deposit Savings at the applicable multiplier
  • Member must remit Regular and Consistent Deposit Savings before and after loan issuance.
  • Flexible Interest rate of 1.3% per month on Reducing Balance.
  • Acceptable Loan Security; Memeber Deposit guarantee or chattels
  • Minimum Guarantors required: 3 Actively saving members.
  • Maximum repayment period: 48 MONTHS.
  • Loan can be Refinanced or Rescheduled to meet members growing needs.
  • Maximum Loan Amount: Upto X3 Member's Deposit Savings.

    Super Development Loan

    This is a credit product designed to meet member's needs to execute and undertake capital-intensive projects and investments. It is designed to be Affordable, Reliable & Customer-Centric.

    Terms and Conditions
  • Maximum Amount: Up to x4 of Member Deposits.
  • Minimum Amount: Ksh. 1,000,000. (1M)
  • Interest Rate: 1% Per Month on Reducing Balance.
  • Maximum repayment Period: 60 Months.(5 YEARS)
  • Minimum Deposits: KSH 250,000/-
  • Loan Security/Collateral: Chattels only i.e, land, cars, buildings and houses, etc
  • Can be paid via the digital banking, check-off arrangement or through bank standing order
  • Loan security must be perfected before admission for use.
  • Minimum share capital Ksh. 20,000
  • Can be Refinanced

    Plot Loan

    This is a credit product designed to fulfil the member's shelter and real estate investments. This product will enable members to acquire parcels of land at very affordable market rates.

    Terms and Conditions
  • Maximum Amount: x3 of Member Deposits
  • Interest Rate: 1.3% Per Month on reducing balance.
  • Maximum repayment period: 36 Months
  • Loan Security/Collateral: Both Chattels and Member Deposit Guarantee acceptable
  • Minimum Guarantors required if member deposits used as collateral: 3
  • The plot may act as collateral to the loan.
  • Can be paid via the digital banking, check-off arrangement or through bank standing order
  • Can be refinanced. Attracts 7.5% fees on outstanding balance

    Instant Loan

    This is a credit product designed for instant consumption. This product avails the option for a member to apply for a loan within their savings and self-guarantee themselves to finance both short term and long term goals and projects.

    Terms and Conditions
  • Maximum Amount: Up to 80% of Member Deposits not exceeding 0.5M
  • Loan Collateral: Self-Guarantee.
  • Disbursement: Approved and Disbursed within 24 Hours.
  • Maximum Repayment Period: 24 Months.
  • Minimum Membership Maturity Period: 6 Months
  • Interest Rate: 1.3-1.5% on reducing balance.
  • Can be refinanced at 7.5% fees

    Asset Financing Loan

    This is a loan product that enables our members to finance and acquire assets such as household items, office equipment, tools of work, machinery and equipment, etc. It is a loan that improves the quality of life for our members.

    Terms and Conditions.
  • Maximum Amount: Up to x3 of Member Deposits but not exceeding Ksh 100,000.
  • Maximum repayment period: 12 Months.
  • Interest Rate: 1.5% per month on reducing balance.
  • Loan Collateral: Minimum of 3 Guarantors or asset itself.
  • Membership period: Not less than 6 months.
  • Can be paid via the digital banking, check-off arrangement or through bank standing order
  • Cheque is done directly to the Vendor/Supplier.
  • Requires an invoice to be attached during application.

    Emergency Loan

    This is a loan product designed to meet member emergencies such as hospital bills, school fees shortfalls, recovery from natural disaster,etc. The product is there to cushion members during difficulties.

    Terms and Conditions
  • Maximum Loan Amount: KSH 30,000.
  • Maximum repayment period: 12 Months.
  • Interest Rate: 1.3-1.5% per month on reducing balance.
  • Loan Collateral: Minimum of 3 Guarantors.
  • Can be paid via the digital banking, check-off arrangement or through bank standing order

    School Fees Loan

    This is a loan product designed to solve the school fees challenges by allowing members to plan for the educational needs of thier children or themselves in advance.

    Terms and Conditions
  • Maximum Loan Amount: Up to x3 of Member Deposits but limited by the fees structure.
  • Repayment Period: Maximum of 12 Months.
  • Interest Rate: 1.3-1.5% per month on reducing balance.
  • Loan Collateral: Minimum of 3 Guarantors.
  • Must attach Fees Structure during application.
  • Cheque is done direct to school.
  • Can be paid via the digital banking, check-off arrangement or through bank standing order
